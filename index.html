Why Strawberry Refresher?

  • Speed of Service: Half the prep time means 2x customer throughput during class breaks.
  • Safe Investment: Low rejection rate ensures broad appeal among the 90% student clientele.
  • Operational Simplicity: No specialized training or expensive whisking equipment required.
  • High Competition: Existing market presence requires strong brand positioning.

Matcha Risks

  • Bottleneck Risk: Long prep times lead to long lines and student dissatisfaction.
  • Polarizing Flavor: 1 out of 3 customers strongly dislikes the product.
  • High Margin: Excellent $3.60 profit per unit.

Final Recommendation: Strawberry Refresher

For Perk Coffee's 50 campus locations, efficiency is the primary driver of success. We recommend the Strawberry Refresher to ensure students reach their classes on time while enjoying a refreshing, universally liked product that streamlines kitchen operations.
