Matcha Insights

  • Viral Trend: 75M+ Social Media mentions.
  • High Loyalty: 32% of customers "Love it".
  • Operational Risk: Slow prep time (2 mins).
  • Polarizing: 33% of customers "Hate it".

Strawberry Insights

  • Efficiency: Fastest prep time (1 min).
  • Safe Bet: Low rejection rate (9% dislike).
  • Lower Margin: $0.70 less profit per cup.
  • High Competition: Found in all nearby cafes.

Strategic Recommendation

Based on the pilot study and market trends, the Matcha Latte offers the highest growth potential and brand differentiation, provided that operational bottlenecks are mitigated through pre-mixing techniques.
